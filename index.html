Projekt Egg

Projekt Egg is a melee fighting game that doesn't use health points. Instead the player chooses in what direction their character will perform a swing and the game tracks where the player's sword hits. If the sword hits one of the enemy's limbs that limb is rendered useless. The enemy dies if it is missing any of the following:

  • Its dominant arm
  • Both of its legs
  • Its head
  • Certain crucial parts of its upper torso
